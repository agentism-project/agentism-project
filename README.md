# Agentism Project

**"Productive agents for universal human well-being."**

Agentism is a socio-economic and political framework designed for the **post-labor era**, where **autonomous agents** (AI, robotics, algorithms) take over the majority of productive work, and the benefits are **redistributed equitably to all human beings**.

This repository is the **official home** of the Agentism initiative — housing its **core documents**, **technical frameworks**, and **collaborative tools**.

---

## 📜 What you'll find here

- **Manifesto**: The foundational vision and principles of Agentism.
- **Methodology**: How Agentism is built, maintained, and communicated.
- **Philosophy**: The humanistic and ethical foundations behind the system.
- **Technical Notes**: Early-stage proposals for the governance and redistribution architecture.
- **Roadmap**: Planned steps, from pilot projects to scalable implementation.
- **Prototypes**: Early simulations and tools exploring Agentism’s mechanisms.

## 🌍 Vision

> Agentism does not seek to break with the past — it seeks to **redirect society** according to the social and technological advances of our time.  
> The goal: a society where **agents work, humans flourish**, and prosperity is shared by all.
