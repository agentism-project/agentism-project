# Principles

This directory contains the five core principles of Agentism — the
**Immutable (Layer 1)** foundation of the project. These principles
define what Agentism IS. They can only be changed through a supermajority
vote (>80%) after a 60-day public discussion period.

## The Five Core Principles

| ID | Title | Summary | Status |
|----|-------|---------|--------|
| [CP-001](CP-001-universal-entitlement.md) | Universal Entitlement to Agent-Generated Prosperity | Every human being has an inherent right to benefit from agent-generated wealth. | DRAFT |
| [CP-002](CP-002-deliberate-transition.md) | Deliberate Transition by Collective Design | The shift to an agent-based society must be deliberate — not an accident of market dynamics. | DRAFT |
| [CP-003](CP-003-human-agency.md) | Human Agency and Purpose Beyond Labor | Human identity and dignity must not depend on economic productivity. | DRAFT |
| [CP-004](CP-004-transparency.md) | Transparency and Accountability of Power | All systems governing autonomous agents must be transparent and accountable. | DRAFT |
| [CP-005](CP-005-regional-autonomy.md) | Regional Autonomy Within Universal Rights | Regions may adapt the framework freely, provided they do not violate CP-001–CP-004. | DRAFT |

## Layer 1: Immutable

These documents are classified as **Layer 1 (Immutable)** in the
[Three-Layer Flexibility Architecture](../governance/decision-making.md).

To change a Layer 1 principle:
1. Open a formal RFC issue
2. 60-day public discussion period
3. Vote requiring >80% supermajority (quorum: 50% of active contributors)
4. 60-day cooling period before implementation

The total number of Immutable principles must never exceed 7.

## Current Status

All principles are in **DRAFT** status during Phase 0. Community review
and formal ratification are planned for Phase 1. If you have feedback,
open a discussion in the [Governance](../governance/) category or submit
a proposal using [governance/proposals/TEMPLATE.md](../governance/proposals/TEMPLATE.md).
